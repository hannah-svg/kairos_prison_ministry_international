Design Challenge

Identifying the design challenge using Human Centered Design methods.

Questionnaire

What is the problem that needs solving?

To change hearts and transform the lives of those inside and outside prison through volunteer prison ministry.

1) The Initial Design Question

How does the Kairos Prison Ministry website garner more volunteers and donations?

2) Ultimate Impact

To touch and transform the lives of those affected by prison.

3) Possible Solutions

  • Hierarchy of information; more contrast
  • Clear navigation
  • What "Kairos" means
  • Brief intro of what the ministry does
  • Specification of audience
  • Effortless appeal for prayer, volunteers, & donations
  • Continuity of design and brand across pages
  • Call to action

4) Context and Constraints

Too much information (altogether over 30 pages and subpages). Minimal focus on men in Kairos Outside program; emphasis on women. Unclear how to get practically connected in volunteer service.

5) The Design Question Tweaked

How can the Kairos website use a clear hierarchy of comprehensively brief information and ordered navigational tools to specify their audience, promote donations, and garner volunteers to address the needs of the incarcerated?
