Kairos Prison Ministry is an international non-profit devoted to ministering to prisoners, incarcerated youth, and their families.

About

As one of the largest international prison ministries in the world, Kairos seeks to change hearts, transform lives, and impact the world. Kairos draws their mission statement and base their service on the foundation of Matthew 25:36 where Jesus says, "I was in prison and you visited me." Additionally, the home page is designed to draw attention to three specific action points: Prayer, Donatation, and Volunteerism.

In line with their threefold mission, Kairos Prison Ministry is organized into three programs: Kairos Inside, Kairos Outside, and Kairos Torch.
